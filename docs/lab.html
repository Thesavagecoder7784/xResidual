Market microstructure

The market lab, where price forms.

Two prediction markets, Kalshi and Polymarket, recorded tick by tick through every World Cup match: full order books and every trade. The question isn't who wins, it's how the price moves, who moves first, what drives it, and whether the obvious in-play edges survive once you cross the spread. Findings reported with their sample sizes, edge or not.
Findings
Each is pooled across every captured match. Where n is small it is shown; read those as indicative. Method and citations are in the research note.
