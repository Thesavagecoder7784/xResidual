What each team drew: its three group games scored by heat risk from stadium and local kickoff time (colour key at right; load is out of 9, each game adding 0 to 3). FIFPRO names six US/Mexico cities extreme heat-injury risk for afternoon kickoffs. European teams draw afternoon kickoffs far more than everyone else. The reason is TV: a US afternoon is European prime-time, exactly the heat window. Exposure only, never a model input. And it intensifies in the knockouts: the afternoon-in-extreme-heat share doubles (14% → 28%).
