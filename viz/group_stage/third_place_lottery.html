Which group sends its third through?

New in 2026: the 8 best third-placed teams join the 24 group qualifiers in the Round of 32. Only one team finishes third in each group — so each bar's segments are the rival candidates for that single slot, and its length is the chance that group's third is good enough to survive. Eight of the twelve will.
