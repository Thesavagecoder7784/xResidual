Who won the draw?

Re-running the actual draw thousands of times (real pots and confederation rules) against a fair re-draw, scored on odds to reach the Round of 32. The format's safety net cushions the strong, so the real victims are bubble teams (Tunisia -23) while soft-draw minnows gain most (Bosnia +24, Egypt +21). The % is each team's reach-R32 odds; the bar is the luck.
