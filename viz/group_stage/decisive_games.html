09 Match Leverage · xResidual

The games that actually decide it

Not the glamour ties, these are the matches whose result swings a team's fate the most. Leverage = how far each side's chance of advancing moves between winning and losing this one game. The midtable six-pointers top the list.
Method · leverage = avg change in P(advance), win vs loss  •  Elo + Poisson group-stage Monte Carlo, 40,000 sims
