Does winning your group pay?

A format is "incentive incompatible" when a team is better off not winning (Csató et al.). The 2026 bracket is fixed, so finishing 1st vs 2nd sends you down different paths. Each dot pair is the expected opponent Elo over your first two knockout rounds (R32 + projected R16). Mostly winning pays, but in Groups C and A the runner-up draws the kinder path — Brazil's group winner faces a ~48-Elo-tougher opening than finishing second.
