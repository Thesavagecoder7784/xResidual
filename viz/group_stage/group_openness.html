The 48-team format advances 32 of 48: the top two of every group, plus the 8 best third-placed teams. So the drama isn't 1st vs 2nd — it's who clears the cut. Solid bar = advance secured by a top-2 finish; light = the slice riding the third-place lottery. A group is wide open when 3+ teams are genuinely alive.
